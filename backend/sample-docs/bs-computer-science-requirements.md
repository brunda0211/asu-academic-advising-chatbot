## Free Electives (19 credits)

Any courses to reach 120 total credits. Students are encouraged to pursue a minor or certificate program.

---

## Academic Policies

- **Minimum Grade:** All CS core courses require a grade of C or better.
- **Repeat Policy:** A course may be repeated once. The higher grade is used in GPA calculation.
- **Academic Probation:** Students with a major GPA below 2.00 for two consecutive semesters will be placed on academic probation.
- **Time to Degree:** Students are expected to complete the degree within 6 years of initial enrollment.
- **Transfer Credits:** Maximum of 64 credits from a community college. All upper-division CS courses must be taken at ASU.

## Suggested 4-Year Plan

### Year 1 (Freshman)
- Fall: ENG 101, MAT 265, CSE 110, General Studies elective
- Spring: ENG 102, MAT 266, CSE 205, MAT 243

### Year 2 (Sophomore)
- Fall: MAT 267, CSE 230, CSE 310, PHY 121
- Spring: STP 281, CSE 340, CSE 360, PHY 131

### Year 3 (Junior)
- Fall: CSE 330, CSE 355, Technical Elective, HU elective
- Spring: CSE 450, CSE 460, Technical Elective, SB elective

### Year 4 (Senior)
- Fall: CSE 485, Technical Elective, Technical Elective, Free Elective
- Spring: CSE 486, Technical Elective, CSE 468, Free Electives
